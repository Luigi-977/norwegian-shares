What Are Shares?

Norwegian Shares — Your stake in the company

A share represents partial ownership of our company. When you buy a share, you become a co-owner. As the company grows, so does the value of your share. All shares are fully registered under Norwegian law.

We offer multiple share classes, each with clear pricing and real-time market data so you always know exactly what your investment is worth.
