What Are Shares?

Norwegian Shares — Your stake in the company

A share represents partial ownership of our company. When you buy a share, you become a co-owner. As the company grows, so does the value of your share. Our shares are fully registered under Norwegian law, giving you legal protection and transparency.

We offer multiple share classes, each with clear pricing and real-time market data so you always know exactly what your investment is worth.
